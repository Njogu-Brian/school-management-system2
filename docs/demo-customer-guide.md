# Demo Guide for Customers

This guide walks a prospective user through the live demo with full, realistic data. Every account below uses password `Demo@123`. Follow the steps in order to experience how the system fits together end-to-end.

## 1) Choose a role and sign in
- Super Admin: `admin.demo@school.test`
- Teacher: `teacher.demo@school.test`
- Accountant/Bursar: `accountant.demo@school.test` or `bursar.demo@school.test`
- Parent: `parent.demo@school.test`
- Student: `student.demo@school.test`
- URL: your demo domain (e.g., `https://demo.school.test/`)

### What each role can see
- Super Admin: full system access to all modules, settings, and permissions.
- Teacher: classes/streams, exams, marks, homework/diaries, attendance for assigned students.
- Accountant/Bursar: fees, invoices, payments/receipts, POS, finance reports.
- Parent: children, invoices, payments, announcements/communications.
- Student: own dashboard, attendance, homework, report cards, announcements.

## 2) Explore the core journey (as Super Admin)
1) Dashboard: review widgets for student counts, fees, attendance, exams, announcements.
2) Students:
   - Go to `Students → Students`, open a learner (Kenyan names included).
   - Check guardians/family, class/stream, category, attendance snippet, and attached documents.
3) Academics:
   - `Academics → Exams`: open the seeded CAT exam and view marks per student.
   - `Academics → Report Cards`: see generated report entries.
   - `Academics → Homework/Diaries`: view homework instructions and diary notes to students.
   - `Academics → Classes/Streams/Subjects`: confirm classes, streams, and core subjects.
4) Attendance:
   - `Attendance → Student Attendance`: view the recorded present entries for the demo day.
5) Fees:
   - `Finance → Fee Structures`: open the demo structure with voteheads and charges.
   - `Finance → Invoices`: open a student invoice; notice partial payment status.
   - `Finance → Payments/Receipts`: open the M-Pesa demo payment/receipt details.
   - `Finance → Voteheads & Categories`: see prefilled categories and voteheads.
6) POS (School Shop):
   - `POS → Products`: open the PE Kit item; see size variants and stock.
   - `POS → Orders`: open the paid order; review items, totals, and paid status.
   - Observe inventory deduction tied to the order.
7) Inventory & Requisitions:
   - `Inventory → Items`: view exercise books, PE kit, lab goggles with stock levels.
   - `Inventory → Transactions`: see GRN (inbound) and issue (outbound) entries.
   - `Inventory → Requisitions`: open the approved requisition for lab goggles; see issued qty.
   - `Inventory → Requirements`: open the requirement template; student requirement marked complete.
8) HR & Payroll:
   - `HR → Staff`: open a staff profile; see employment fields and contact info.
   - `HR → Payroll → Periods`: open current period; see totals.
   - Open a payroll record: allowances, deductions, net pay, and generated payslip number.
9) Transport:
   - `Transport → Routes/Vehicles/Trips`: see the “Nairobi Westlands Loop” route, vehicle `KDA 234A`, and the morning trip.
   - Check a student assignment with pickup/drop-off points.
10) Hostel:
    - `Hostel → Hostels/Rooms/Allocations`: open “Kilimanjaro Hostel”, room A1, and its active allocation.
11) Library:
    - `Library → Books`: open “Fasihi ya Kiswahili”; see copies and availability.
    - `Borrowings`: open the active borrowing tied to the library card.
12) Communication & Announcements:
    - `Communication → Templates`: open “Fee Reminder” SMS template with placeholders.
    - `Announcements`: see “Welcome to Demo Mode” published and visible on dashboard.
13) Documents:
    - From a student profile: open the sample report document.
    - From a staff profile: open the HR contract sample.
14) Settings & Permissions:
    - `Settings → Roles/Permissions`: view roles (admin, teacher, bursar, accountant, parent, student) and confirm super admin has full control.

## 3) Experience the Parent view
- Sign in as `parent.demo@school.test`.
- Home: see children listed.
- Invoices/Payments: open a child’s invoice and payment history.
- Announcements/Communications: view messages that apply to guardians.

## 4) Experience the Student view
- Sign in as `student.demo@school.test`.
- Dashboard: personal summary.
- Attendance: view daily status.
- Academics: view homework and report cards for the student.
- Announcements: see any published notices.

## 5) Notes and safety
- Demo data is preloaded and safe to explore. Feel free to open, filter, and read records.
- Avoid deleting seeded records; creating new ones for testing is fine.
- If any page appears empty, ask the team to refresh the demo data (we reseed regularly).
